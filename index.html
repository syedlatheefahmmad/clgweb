About PVPSIT

Prasad V. Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) is a prestigious engineering college located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. It is affiliated with JNTUK and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. The institution is known for its excellent faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and vibrant campus life.
